Android

APK direct install · Android 5.0+

  • 1

    Go to Settings → Security and enable Install unknown apps for your browser or file manager.

  • 2

    Tap the button below — it will download the APK directly to your phone.

  • 3

    Open your Downloads folder, tap the .apk file, then tap Install. Done!

iPhone / iPad

iOS 12+ · Requires special distribution

  • 1

    TestFlight (recommended): The developer shares a TestFlight invite link — tap it on your iPhone to install the app directly.

  • 2

    Ad-hoc / Enterprise: If you received an .ipa file, install it then go to Settings → General → VPN & Device Management to trust the profile.

  • 3

    App Store: Once published, search "AgriApp" in the App Store and install normally.

🔗  Join TestFlight (iOS)
iOS note: Apple requires a paid Developer Account ($99/yr) to distribute outside the App Store. Contact the developer to be added as a tester via TestFlight.
